The Method

How The Grief Recovery Method Works

A structured, action-based approach — not endless talk therapy. Most people complete the process in 7–8 sessions.

1

Discover Your Losses

Complete the Loss History Graph — a powerful visual map of every significant loss in your life and your emotional reactions.

2

Learn the Actions

Guided by a certified specialist, learn the specific action steps for processing and completing your relationship to loss.

3

Complete the Work

Work through structured exercises that help you say the things you never got to say and release emotional pain.

4

Move Forward

Experience emotional completeness — a measurable shift from pain to peace that opens you up to life again.
