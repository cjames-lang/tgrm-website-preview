Our Story
A Method Born from Loss

In 1977, John W. James lost his infant son. In the devastating aftermath, he discovered that everything society had taught him about dealing with grief was wrong. "Time heals all wounds." "Be strong." "Keep busy." None of it worked.

Instead of accepting that grief was something to endure, John began developing a set of action-based tools — specific steps anyone could take to move beyond their pain. He partnered with Russell Friedman, and together they created The Grief Recovery Method.

"Action heals grief, not time."

What began as one father's search for relief has grown into a global movement. Today, over 3,000 Certified Grief Recovery Specialists in 20+ countries use this method to help people move beyond loss.
