Inside the Book
What You'll Discover
A step-by-step action program that has helped over a million grievers move from pain to recovery.
🚫

Myths About Grief

Identify the six most common myths that keep grievers stuck — like "time heals all wounds" and "be strong" — and learn why they don't work.

📋

The Action Steps

A clear, sequential set of action steps designed to help you complete the pain caused by loss, rather than just cope with it.

🔍

Your Relationship Review

A guided process for examining your relationship with the person or situation you've lost — the good, the bad, and the unfinished.

📝

The Completion Letter

Learn to write and deliver the letter that says the things you wish you'd said — apologies, forgiveness, and significant emotional truths.

📖

Beyond the Handbook

How to apply the Grief Recovery Method to future losses and continue growing emotionally long after completing the program.

❤

For Every Type of Loss

Death, divorce, job loss, health crises, estrangement, pet loss — the Handbook addresses over 40 types of grief-producing events.
