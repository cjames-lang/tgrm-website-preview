Your Host

Cole James

President, The Grief Recovery Institute · Author · Speaker

Cole W. James is the President of the Grief Recovery Institute and host of the Feel Free Again podcast. As the son of founder John W. James, Cole grew up immersed in the world of grief recovery and was encouraged to be “a heart with ears” from a very early age.

Through the podcast, Cole brings the same compassion and practical wisdom to listeners everywhere — sharing real stories, honest conversations, and evidence-based tools that help people break free from the prison of unresolved grief.
