Community Guidelines

A Safe Space, Not Just a Slogan

Our community is moderated daily by trained grief recovery professionals. Here's what we promise:

  • 💚
    No toxic positivity. You'll never be told to "look on the bright side." Your pain is real and valid.
  • 🙈
    No unsolicited advice. Members support with empathy, not with "you should..." statements.
  • 🔒
    Confidential by default. What's shared in the community stays in the community.
  • 🙋
    Zero tolerance for judgment. No gatekeeping grief. All types of loss are valid. No exceptions.
  • 💪
    Action over talk. We focus on the tools and actions that lead to actual recovery — not endless venting without direction.
CJPresident & Owner, GRI
