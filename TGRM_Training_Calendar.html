Common Questions About Training

Can I choose Foundational or Advanced on the same training date? +
Yes — for all online training dates, you can enroll as either Foundational ($2,495) or Advanced ($3,995). Both tracks train together in the same cohort for the core curriculum, and Advanced participants receive additional training hours, lesson plans, and marketing resources. In-person dates are offered exclusively as the Premium In-Person Certification ($4,995), which includes everything in the Advanced track plus hands-on coaching, live roleplay, and direct mentorship.
What happens on training days? +
Each day runs from 9am to 5pm with breaks. You'll learn the GRM methodology through a mix of instruction, demonstration, guided practice, and Q&A. Online sessions use Zoom with cameras on for an interactive, small-group experience.
Can I upgrade from Foundational to Advanced later? +
Absolutely. If you start with the Foundational certification, you can upgrade to Advanced at any time by paying the difference. Many specialists choose to start Foundational and add the Advanced training once they've built their practice.
What if I need to reschedule my training? +
We understand life happens. You can transfer to a different training date up to 14 days before your session at no additional cost. Within 14 days, a $200 transfer fee applies. Contact our team to arrange a transfer.
Do I need a degree or license to enroll? +
No. The GRM certification is open to anyone with a passion for helping others—counselors, coaches, pastors, nurses, educators, HR professionals, and caring individuals from all backgrounds.
