Common Questions

How is this different from a PESI or Evergreen grief certification? +
Those are excellent continuing education programs, and many of our specialists hold them too. The difference: those programs teach you about grief. The Grief Recovery Method certifies you to deliver a complete, structured program to clients—with ready-made lesson plans, homework assignments, and a step-by-step process you can start offering immediately. Plus, we're the only grief program with independent, evidence-based validation from Kent State University.
Can I really be effective after just 4 days? +
You won't be an expert in 4 days—we'd never claim that. But you will leave with a solid foundation, a proven framework, and all the materials to begin helping people right away. More importantly, you'll have experienced the method yourself by working on one of your own losses during the training. That personal experience is what transforms good helpers into great ones. And you'll have a full year of unlimited support from our team as you build your confidence.
Do I need a degree or license to enroll? +
No. The Grief Recovery Method is an educational program, not psychotherapy. Our community includes licensed therapists, social workers, hospice nurses, chaplains, funeral directors, coaches, and people whose own grief experience ignited a passion to help others. What you need is a genuine desire to help and the willingness to do your own grief work during the training.
Is this approved for CE credits? +
The Grief Recovery Method Certification Training is approved for Veteran's Education Benefits and may be used for continuing education credit with the Association of Professional Chaplains. For other license types, we recommend checking with your state board, as continuing education acceptance varies. Many licensed professionals attend our training as a complement to their required CE programs because it provides practical tools they can't get elsewhere.
What happens after I'm certified? +
You're not on your own. You'll be listed in our global referral directory where grievers search for help, you'll have your own microsite on griefrecoverymethod.com, 150+ marketing videos to attract clients, access to our Specialist Resource Community for ongoing learning, and a full year of unlimited one-on-one support via email, phone, and Zoom. We don't just certify you. We invest in your success.
