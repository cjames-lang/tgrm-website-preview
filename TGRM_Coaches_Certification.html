Grief Isn't Just About Death and Divorce

People grieve over anything or anyone they've lost. And how they cope with these emotional changes impacts every choice they make — including the goals they bring to you.

Job loss, business failure, health changes, broken trust, missed promotions, shattered dreams, estrangement — these are all grief events. And they're probably showing up in your coaching sessions right now.

The problem isn't that your clients lack motivation. It's that unresolved grief is the invisible block sitting between where they are and where they want to be. Until that grief is addressed, no amount of goal-setting, accountability, or mindset work will create lasting change.
💼

Client still hurting from losing a job?

Disappointment and anger make it hard to muster the energy to look for a new job or consider a new career.

📈

Client struggling to turn around a business?

Worrying over recent performance and fear of further losses can be debilitating.

💔

Client broken-hearted from a relationship ending?

Fear from the past keeps people from opening to new love and new possibilities.

😱

Client afraid to try new things after past failure?

Regret holds people back from exploring new possibilities and taking meaningful risks.

🩹

Client struggling with health issues?

Frustration and fear make it hard to see the light at the end of the tunnel.
