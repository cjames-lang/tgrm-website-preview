About Sarah

With over 15 years of experience as a Licensed Clinical Social Worker, Sarah Mitchell noticed a recurring gap in her clinical training: while she had been taught to help people manage symptoms of grief, she hadn't been given the tools to help them complete it.

After discovering the Grief Recovery Method, Sarah became certified in 2018 and has since facilitated more than 200 programs for individuals, families, and groups. Her approach is warm, direct, and action-oriented — she believes grief doesn't need to be a life sentence, and that the right tools can help anyone move from pain to a place of peace.

Sarah specializes in working with adults and children who are navigating loss from death, divorce, pet loss, career transitions, and health crises. She offers both in-person sessions in Portland and virtual programs for clients nationwide.
