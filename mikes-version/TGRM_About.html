The Method

  • Action-based, not talk-based — Focuses on doing, not dwelling, to move grief from the head to resolution
  • Completes unfinished emotional communication — Addresses the things we wish we'd said or done
  • Works for 40+ types of loss — Death, divorce, career changes, relationships, identity shifts, and more
  • 7-week structured program — Guided, time-limited, and designed for measurable progress
  • Validated by peer-reviewed research — Kent State University confirmed statistically significant outcomes

Our Story

1984
John W. James founded the Grief Recovery Institute after experiencing profound loss. He created a method that transformed his own grief—and has since helped millions worldwide.
1998
The Grief Recovery Handbook is published, bringing evidence-based grief recovery tools into homes and offices across the world.
2014
Kent State University validates the method through peer-reviewed research, confirming statistically significant improvements in grief outcomes.
2024
Over 10,000 certified specialists worldwide are trained and equipped to bring evidence-based grief recovery to their clients and communities.

"We believe no one should have to grieve alone, and no professional should have to face a griever without proper tools."
