About the Grief Recovery Method®

40+ Years of Evidence-Based Grief Recovery

Founded in 1977, we've helped millions find their way through loss with action-based recovery.

A Method Born from Loss

The Grief Recovery Method was founded by John W. James following the tragic loss of his son. Rather than accepting the notion that "time heals all wounds," John set out to discover what actually helps people move through grief.

In partnership with Russell Friedman, John developed the first action-based grief recovery program—not a support group, not therapy, but a concrete set of steps that address the root causes of prolonged grief.

What started as one man's personal journey became a transformative approach now recognized globally. Today, 10,000+ certified specialists carry this mission forward, helping individuals, families, and organizations heal.

Our core belief remains unchanged: Action heals grief, not time.
