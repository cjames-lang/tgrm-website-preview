Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a clinical license to become a Grief Recovery Specialist?

+
No. Grief Recovery Specialists come from all walks of life. Whether you're a mental health professional, spiritual counselor, coach, or someone called to help others through grief, you can certify. Our curriculum is designed to be accessible and practical for anyone with a genuine desire to support others.

What's the difference between online and in-person training?

+
The curriculum is identical—you'll receive the same training, materials, and certification. The main difference is delivery: online trainings are conducted live via Zoom (you can participate from anywhere), while in-person trainings bring the cohort together in our designated location. Both formats include interactive exercises, group discussions, and one-on-one support.

Are continuing education units (CEUs) available?

+
Yes. CEU credits are available for most professional licenses including counselors, social workers, nurses, and other healthcare professionals. The specific requirements and number of credits vary by state and license type. We'll provide documentation and guidance to help you report your credits to your licensing board.

What if I need to reschedule my training?

+
We understand life happens. We offer a flexible rescheduling policy—if you need to move your dates, just let us know at least 14 days before your training begins. You can reschedule to any upcoming training at no additional cost, subject to availability.

Do you offer payment plans?

+
Yes. We offer flexible payment plans to make certification accessible. Payment plans are available for all trainings, and we work with you to find a schedule that fits your budget. Contact us at 1-818-907-9600 or email info@tgrm.org to discuss payment options.
