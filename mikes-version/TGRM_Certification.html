Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this certification designed for? ➕

This certification is ideal for licensed therapists, counselors, social workers, life coaches, clergy, hospital chaplains, HR professionals, and anyone who works with grieving individuals. You don't need prior grief experience—just a genuine desire to help people through loss.

What's the time commitment for the main certification? ➕

The main certification is a 4-day intensive program (32 hours). This can be completed in consecutive days or spread over 2 weekends, depending on the training schedule. Most participants complete it within one month, then engage in 2-3 months of mentored practice before final certification.

Are CEU credits really included? ➕

Yes! Our main certification program includes 30+ approved CEUs. The credits are approved by multiple licensing boards including NBCC, NASW, APA, and state psychology boards. Advanced program includes 15+ additional CEUs. Your certificate is issued upon completion and exam passage.

Can I take training online, or do I need to attend in-person? ➕

We offer both! Our live online trainings are highly interactive—you'll participate in role-plays, practice facilitation, and get real-time feedback. In-person trainings at our locations across the country offer the same comprehensive experience with additional networking. Choose what works best for your schedule and learning style.

What's included after the training ends? ➕

Your certification includes one year of ongoing support: access to mentored practice sessions, peer consultation calls, continuing education webinars, listing on our Specialist Directory, marketing templates, and access to our private specialist community. We want you to succeed long-term.

What's the difference between online and in-person training? ➕

The curriculum is identical—both formats are comprehensive and rigorous. Online training uses interactive video, breakout rooms for practice, and real-time instructor feedback. In-person training offers the same education plus in-room networking and the ability to practice group facilitation with live participants. Both produce fully certified specialists.

Can I take the Advanced program without the main certification? ➕

No, the Advanced program is designed as a 2-day add-on to the main certification. It dives deeper into specialized populations (military, children, trauma, etc.) and advanced facilitation skills. You'll need main certification first to enroll in Advanced.

Do you offer corporate training discounts? ➕

Absolutely! We offer custom corporate training and team certification programs with volume pricing. Whether you're training 5 people or 50, we can design a program that fits your timeline and budget. Contact our corporate training team for a personalized quote.
